Our Story

It started off with a couple of guys with some spare rooms, a love for video game collecting and a lot of duplicate games they didn't care for, and then came the idea of selling them online, selling them listing by listing on other people's site seemed too silly for such a large quantity of games, and thus the idea came; GameHub!

Our Purpose

Our purpose with GameHub is to give people a way to relive their childhood games, get some money for older games they no longer care for and finding the games new homes.
